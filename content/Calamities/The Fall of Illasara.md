The exact start of the Illasaran empire is heavily disputed, but it is generally agreed the long collapse of this massive elvish empire began with the death of [[Illasara]] themself. At this point the vast empire split into six different kingdoms ruled by each of her children. Each had their capital on [[Illaset]] and some ruled portions of the elvish territory on [[Cyn-Tollir]], but these claims lapsed as they began to fight amongst each other over [[Illaset]].

The land was nearly united under the tyrannical rule of her Erendael before his own empire was struck by calamity. [[Humans]] began appearing on [[The Material Plane]] and raiding the northern shores of [[Illaset]]. The [[Elves]] and their slowly growing populations had little ability to stem the flood of these human raiders. Kingdoms fell and libraries burned as [[Humans]] swept across the continent. Eventually the flood was stemmed, but not before many of Illasara's greatest achievements were erased. A similar story occurred on [[Cyn-Tollir]], across its southern shore.
