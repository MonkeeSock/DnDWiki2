While the first demonic assaults on [[Ackriel Island]] and [[Drazkuvar]] started during [[The War of the Gods]], approximately 500 years ago [[Azurlee]] led a renewed and ferocious assault on [[Ackriel Island]]. Hordes of [[Demons]] assaulted the islands shores and overwhelmed its inhabitants. At the height of the crisis, known as the Year of High Water, [[Ackriel]] himself intervened and turned the tide of the conflict but was wounded in the process.  Several powerful dwarven kingdoms and their vassal states utterly collapsed. 

In the centuries since it the great dwarven kingdoms of old have fallen to absolute ruin. The majority of high elves have fled to the [[Maiden's Necklace]] leaving their wood elf kin behind. Only some human baronies and similar territories have recovered. These humans still swear fealty to [[High King Thanvar]] but have little oversight in practice.
