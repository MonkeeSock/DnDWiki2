Devil's Oak is a species of tree known for its sap that is often used as an ingredient in various poisons. The sap on its own is not toxic enough to be lethal but causes nausea and a burning sensation in the mouth and throat. The sap can also be distilled to create various potions with other fire related effects. 

Devil's Oaks were created in a fiendish ritual now lost to time. There have been attempts both to cultivate and destroy these trees, neither of which have ever been truly successful. It is said that the shaft of [[Dispater]]'s spear is made of Devi'ls Oak.
