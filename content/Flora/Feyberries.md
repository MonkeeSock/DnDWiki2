Feyberries are a white berry that grows on a woody shrub. These shrubs only grow in places where the barrier between [[The Material Plane]] and the [[Feywild]] are thin. These berries are used for a variety of purposes, but most commonly it is used to create an oil which can be used to overcome the magic resistances certain creatures might have.
