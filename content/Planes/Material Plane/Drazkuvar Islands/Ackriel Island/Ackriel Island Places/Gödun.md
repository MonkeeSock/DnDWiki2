Gödun is a town on a peninsula on [[Ackriel Island]]. The people of Gödun have declared themselves independent of their ruling Baron. While no official war has broken out, each side has hired hobgoblin mercenaries to skirmish in the area. 

The town encompasses the peninsula of Gödun's Head and Dubrech Island. Etering the town by land requires crossing the Gödun's Neck. On both sides of the road the neck is marshy. The road itself is heavily fortified by goblinoid mercenaries. Most sea travel enters through the docks on the south side of town and Eiker Bay.

Since independence trade with Tharosis Island has greatly increased. The taverns, hostels and brothels on the south side of sound have swelled swell with sailors and goblinoids. Under the Baron trade in Gödun was heavily taxed but the council of Gödun now refuses to pay, allowing cheaper trade.
