Muntz is a small village on [[Ackriel Island]]. It is in a hilly region. The local residents produce a variety of agricultural products. They raise cattle and grow grain in the village. The village has been under attack by goblins and was the location of [[Ackriel Island Session 1]]. 

Muntz is owned by [[Lord Ivor]].
