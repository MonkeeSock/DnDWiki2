Nisk is often considered to be the last great city of [[Drazkuvar]]. It is by far the largest city across all of the islands and is the economic center of [[Ackriel Island]]. While the city is primarily populated by humans, it is the most diverse city in [[Drazkuvar]] and people of every species may be found there. 

Nisk is densely populated and can be divided in several ways. One of the most important ways is between the inner and outer city. The inner part of the city sits behind an ancient wall and holds the city palace, temples and other of the cities most important civic buildings. In addition, there is dense housing, inns, markets, shops, industrial centers and the city's most busy docks and quays. Overall it is a crowded and diverse mix of working, middle and upper class people of infinite background. 

The outer part of the city can be divided itself into two major districts. North of the river is far more affluent and less much less dense. Some of the city's middle class along with inns and boutique businesses are here but more notable are the villas and estates of local and foreign nobles. The wealthy and powerful folk from other islands in [[Drazkuvar]] have their summer and vacation homes here.

South of the river the dense housing and urban environment seems to spill out of the city's ancient walls. Here is where the majority of the city's working class reside. This is also where large numbers of goblinoid mercenaries are allowed to dwell. By law these mercenaries are only allowed temporary housing so their neighborhoods have become sprawling shanty towns made of cheap and flimsy material. Despite this, large potions of this temporary population have become permanent residents instead, providing services beyond the original scope of mercenary work. 

These goblinoid neighborhoods have been referred to as a colony by some local residents and ethnic tension has not been uncommon.
