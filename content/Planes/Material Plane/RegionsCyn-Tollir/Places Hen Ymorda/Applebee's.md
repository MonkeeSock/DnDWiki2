Applebee's is restaurant chain founded by members of [[The Bandit Bashers]]. The first location was founded in Decharu. Applebee's uses a variation of the enlarge/reduce spell when it slaughters animals to harvest more from them. 

In it's early years the expansion of Applebee's was limited because the magical knowledge required for the business model was uncommon across [[Hen Ymorda]]. To solve this, Applebee's has partnered with [[The Celestial Empire]] to found universities across the land to build the educational foundation required to flourish. In the thirty years since Applebee's founding and it is now entering a new period of expansion.
