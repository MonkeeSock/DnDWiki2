The Order of the Griffon is chivalric order on [[Amprella Island]]. The leader of the order is the king of [[Amprella Island]]. The highest ranking knights in the order become members of the kings guard known as the Imperial Talon. Members of the Imperial Talon have their own griffon mounts they ride into battle. While here are only eight talons there are many lower ranking members of the order.
