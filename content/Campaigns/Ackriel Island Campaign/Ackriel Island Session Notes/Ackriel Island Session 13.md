After fighting the cultists the party found the druid Kelmac at his sanctuary. As they approached his cave the party was overcome with a deep sense of serenity. He was sitting with a hand outstretched and a blackbird was building a nest in his palm. He was able to help direct the party to elvish siblings they were searching for and warn the party that Helena was a hag.

The party continued on into the first and found the primary village of the elves. They learned that the elves here worshipped [[Raven Who Steals the Sun]], the sometimes lover and sometimes rival of [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]]. They met there but she didn't remember the way to the shrine, but believed her brother Aramil might. Aramil had been kidnapped by orcs in the nearby hills. 

[[Violet]] met with the tribe's lorekeeper and learned some new spells. They also got a scout named Fennel to agree to help guide the party to the orc's stronghold. The party set out, nearly having an encounter with a giant boar. [[Knura]] used the spell animal friendship to charm the boar and keep it from attacking the party. 

As the party searched for the orcish stronghold they found a great sword embedded in a stone. Carved in Dwarvish around the stone it said "Only toril's chosen may retrieve the sword from the stone". They learned from [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] and Fennel that Toril was an ancient dwarven king in the area whose hall had been flooded by [[Azurlee]]. Toril's crown was still at the bottom of the flooded hall.
