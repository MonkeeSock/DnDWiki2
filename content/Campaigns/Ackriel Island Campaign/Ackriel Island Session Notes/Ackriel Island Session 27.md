The party was attacked by a banshee in a room full of broken and covered mirrors. AS they fought the banshee was able to enter the mirrors to hide from the party. By uncovering the unbroken mirrors and pointing them at the banshee they were able to do some damage and force the banshee out of the mirror it had entered. 

In the broken mirrors the banshee was able to summon twisted reflections of members pf the party to help fight them. 

After defeating the banshee they decided to continue further in the tower. They came across the skeletons of the previous inhabitants of the tower. They were still and showed no signs of violence, having been sealed away in the ancient siege. 

Eventually they reached an upper floor which had a wall with a large mural depicting Kaz, the elvish lich king who ruled from the tower. The mural showed Kaz at the top of the tower wielding a dark and wicked blade. In elvish there was a caption which ead, "Woe to any who disturb the rest of Kaz the Undying"

The party opened the door to the tomb and came across gleaming piles of treasure. [[Evette]] used her magic sense and detected several magic items: a circlet, a ring, a rod, a cloak and Kaz's sword. [[Violet]] placed a magic circle at the door of the tomb. A skeleton sat on a throne at the end of the room. 

The party entered the room and each grabbed a magic item before booking it to the door. As they ran the skull began to levitate, its eyes glowed and it chased the party. It immediately knocked out [[Valeria Modell]] and [[Percy]] had to retrieve her. 

The magic circle delayed the demilich but it was able to create an antimagic field and continue its pursuit. The party used the bell [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] had given them to delay it as they ran. 

They returned to the party and told people to flee as it followed behind them. As the pparty fled the demilich levitated down from the mezzanine level to the center of the ballroom. [[Eliza Fairsail]], [[Edward Darcy]] and the Hierophant joined the fray. While the party was doing good damage it was clear they were out of their depth and the demilich killed two guests, restoring its lich form. 

The party was able to escape the tower as Kaz began laughing behind them and storm clouds began to gather above the city.
