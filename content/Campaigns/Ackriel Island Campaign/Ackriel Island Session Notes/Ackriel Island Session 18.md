After a rest in [[Muntz]] the part began used the feather from [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] to retrieve the story of the haunted tavern they had seen weeks ago when they first began escorting Crow. The version of the story they got was from the perspective of Garwin, the only one in the tavern the night of a murder sixty years ago. It had been a cold, bitter rainy night, despite which there were few visitors that night. When he woke in the morning the innkeeper Barty was dead outside the door to his daughter's room. his daughter Winnifred was dead inside the room, her head severed. Barty had died from shock at the sight. 

The party was joined by a goliath named Kion. The party spoke with Garwin and confirmed his tale. They also tried to speak with Old Man Zeb, who was friends with Barty and Winni all those years ago. Zebadiah Jr. did not allow them to speak with him because he was feeling unwell that day.

The party went to investigate the inn and arrived as night fell. Outside the inn was ruined and abandoned but when they entered they found a warm room with a blazing hearth, food laden tables and a fat welcoming innkeeper. They realized this was Barty's spirit trapped in the inn, reliving the night of his daughter's and his own death over and over again.

The party rented a room at the inn and began to investigate Winni's room. They found her headless spirit guarding her bed and at one point looking out the window on the second floor. Because being inside the inn recreated the atmosphere of the murder, heavy rain made it hard to see out the window.

Eventually the party found a well outside the inn in the same direction Winni had been loking. Kion used a potion of water breathing and [[Violet]] used alter self to dive into the will which led into an extra dimensional space filled with water. At the bottom of the well they found Winni's head where it had been dumped sixty years ago. 

They returned Winni's head and explained that the night she was murdered she had invited Zebadiah into her room. They were lovers and keeping it secret from her father. As he came into bed withe her he suddenly cut of her head with a short sword. She wanted revenge.

The party went to confront Zebadiah Sr. and him and his son chained in their cider mill by Zebadiah Jr.'s wife. They were both werewolves on the verge of transformation that night. The party waited until the next morning after their transformation and brought them to confront Winnifred. Winnifred wanted to kill Zebadiah Sr. but was convinced by [[Percy]] to allow the party to bring Zebadiah before the law and find justice that way.
