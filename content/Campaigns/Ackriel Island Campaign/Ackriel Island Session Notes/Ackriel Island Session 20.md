The party left the volcano and were greeted by Wren and a half-elf ranger Lia. They were thanked for having dealt with the genie in the mountain and Wren provided more spells for [[Violet]]. The party returned to Muntz and received a letter from [[Lord Ivor]] who had heard of some of their actions in the area and wanted to reward them.

The part returned to [[Nisk]] and visited Ivor's manor but as they approached he front gate there no guards on duty. As they entered the found a trail of blood leading to some bushes where the guards had been hidden after they were killed. As they rushed into the manor they found the rest of the staff and guards had been killed as well.

The party made their way up to Ivor's office and entered just as an elvish woman was stabbing him through the chest. She seemed to recognize the party and invited them to a conversation. She was particularly interested in [[Evette]] and [[Violet]] but dismissive of the rest of the party. Mostly, she seemed interested in things [[Evette]] remembered of her mother. The party learned her name was [[Alorra]].

Eventually, when the party refused to provide her information a fight broke out. The party managed to escape her and the imps with her by jumping out of the window in Ivor's office. [[Violet]] was knocked out but dragged out of the manor complex by [[Evette]]. During the fight [[Violet]]'s use of Tasha's Hideous Laughter kept [[Alorra]] from being able to act most of the fight.
