After retrieving the sword in the stone, flametongue, the party went after [[Helena]] in the swamp. 

As they approached [[Helena]]'s hut they began to feel, strange and malevolent magic in the area. They were watched by [[Helena]]'s familiar, a black cat as they approached. The party used the crow's feather to get personal details about [[Helena]], learning she was the daughter of [[Baba Yaga]]. They also learned of her previous encounters with [[Kelmac]] and how she was reluctant to confront him. 

The party at first tried to negotiate with [[Helena]] for [[Percy]]'s blood but were unsuccessful. [[Violet]] disguised herself as [[Kelmac]] to intimidate her which was a good idea but ultimately unsuccessful. 

A fight broke out and [[Violet]] used mage hand to grab vial from [[Helena]]. Earlier, [[Knura]] had used locate object to pinpoint its location. This was crucial as it prevented [[Helena]] from using the vial against them and controlling [[Percy]]. [[Violet]] nearly opened the vial in combat, which would have been disastrous but stopped. The party was otherwise able to quickly dispatch [[Helena]], but when they defeated her all that was left was a puddle of water. They had defeated a simulacrum of [[Helena]] rather than the hag herself. 

In her crocodile form [[Knura]] smashed the vial activating the rune on it and absorbing a large amount of damage. The party decided not to pursue [[Helena]] in her lair.
