The session began with [[Violet]] waking with a cough. She was tied to a rustic a before a poorly ventilated fireplace inside of a chamber in the goblin lair. Across from her sat [[Alkar]], the wizard leading the goblin band. He was drinking from a goblet and flipping through the pages of her spell book. 

As [[Alkar]] realized [[Violet]] was awake he explained she was not in danger, for now, and that he wanted ransom her and the other players back to [[Violet]]'s family. He began to press her for details as to what her and the party were doing in the area and who had sent them to the lair. [[Violet]] feigned amnesia as a result of the recent battle during the part's assault on the lair. 

[[Alkar]] sent [[Violet]] back to the temporary holding cell in the cave where her and the party were able to converse in whispers. She was blindfolded on her way back from [[Alkar]]'s room but managed to remember the path back from the cells. She also met there a new character, [[Knura]], a druid who held a strange resemblance to the fallen [[Nura]]. [[Knura]] had been sent by [[Lord Ivor]], [[Alkar]]'s former employer, to hunt down and kill the wizard. She was captured in the process. 

As the rest of the party woke from their captivity they began to plot their escape from captivity. They managed to overhear [[Alkar]] in conversation with someone else, a man with an infernal bent to his accent. He seemed to be in charge of [[Alkar]] and sent him on a "special assignment". The tiefling stranger also promised to send [[Alkar]] reinforcements. 

After the stranger left [[Knura]] used her wild shape ability to scout the cave and located the party's captured equipment. She began by moving left three times and ending up in [[Alkar]]'s office. This was the one place in the cave that [[Violet]] had already seen and could confirm didn't hold there equipment. [[Knura]] explored the rest of the cave and located the equipment as well as getting an accurate count of the amount of goblins present. 

After the party confirmed that [[Alkar]] had left [[Kharmon]] used his skills to convince their goblin guard, Guzzick, to let them out of their shackles. After some convincing, Guzzick let the party out and left with them in the night. The party recovered their equipment and searched [[Alkar]]'s office, leaning about his feud with [[Lord Ivor]]. [[Alkar]] had made a necklace of charm person which Ivor used to secure a prosperous marriage and, according to [[Alkar]], sleep with [[Alkar]]'s wifie Olivia. 

In [[Muntz]] the party and Guzzick went sperate ways before meeting with the mayor. They updated him on the situation and secured additional gold for eliminating [[Alkar]] and the coming reinforcements. They also investigated what rumors they could find about [[Alkar]] and his wife Olivia, learning there had been a significant age gap in the relationship. 

The party returned to the goblin cave and slaughtered the remaining forces in their sleep. They then prepared for the coming reinforcements. [[Violet]] made a trap out of the remaining fear the goblins had in the cave at the chokepoint where the entrance met the cave's main chamber. She, [[Evette]] and [[Kharmon]] hid in the cave while [[Knura]] remained outside as a bear, planning to cut off the goblins exit. With everything in place the goblins and [[Alkar]] arrrived and the combat was saved for the next session.
