The party began on the road to [[Muntz]], a village on [[Ackriel Island]]. They had been hired by the mayor of the village to handle a goblin band that was threatening the village. On the way to the village they encountered a young man travelling to the same village on a cart with an empty chest pulled by a stubborn donkey. After a brief conversation with the man, named Gordy, they decided to travel with him the rest of the way to the village. 

Along the way the party was ambushed by a group of goblins at a bend in the road. [[Evette]] absorbed a lot of the damage from the ambushing goblins and took several down with her halberd. [[Kharmon]] effectively used magic missile on some of the goblins and [[Violet]] was able to use her cantrips against other goblins. [[Nura]] moved into melee to support [[Evette]], and Gordy hid under the cart. 

The ambush was quickly over, with two goblins dead, one escaping and the fourth one captured by the party. This fourth one was the leader of the raid and second in command of the goblins attacking [[Muntz]]. The party interrogated him and learned his name was Grizzick. Through his broken common they learned some details about the location of the goblin base, the number of goblins and their motivation (money). With the promise of his life being spared and the potential for additional coin Grizzizk joined the party. 

They continued onto [[Muntz]] to investigate the problem. As they arrived at the village [[Kharmon]] disguised Grizzick as a halfling and the party entered the village. Gordy returned to his father's home while [[Kharmon]], Grizzick and [[Nura]] went to the tavern. [[Evette]] and [[Violet]] went on to the mayor's house. 

In the tavern [[Kharmon]], Grizzick and [[Nura]] tried to gather intel from the one local patron and the innkeeper. They learned some details about how the goblins had been impacting the village, disrupting trade and raiding cattle injuring some villagers in the process. Generally, the local militia had been able to handle the goblins so far but it seemed the problem was beginning to escalate. During the intel gathering process [[Kharmon]] was challenged to drinking game, which he ultimately lost. 

At the mayor's house [[Violet]] and [[Evette]] learned more details about the impact the goblins had been having and some guesses as to the location of their lair. They also secured 100 gold up front, in addition to the 200 they had been promised when they accepted the job. 

They began the next day by visiting the village's general store to purchase potions of healing before attacking the goblin's lair. [[Kharmon]] was able to get a discount on the potions which allowed the party to boy one potion for each member. Afterwards, they moved on to the goblins' layer, guided by Grizzick. 

As they traveled through the hills they spotted and were spotted by a goblin on watch. [[Evette]] chased and tackled the goblin and with Grizzick as translator they managed to gain some information from him. 

The party learned more about how many goblins were part of this particular band and who their leader was. They learned the leader of these goblins was a human, rather than another goblin. They intimidated the goblin into gathering some of the other goblins and bringing them to place the party could ambush them. 

The party set up their ambush and as the goblins arrived they made quick work of the encounter. [[Kharmon]] destroyed multiple goblins at once with a magic missile spell and the party made sure none of the goblins escaped the encounter. 

The party approached the cave the goblins were using as their layer. It opened into a cliff face beside a waterfall and they learned from Grizzick there was a secret entrance into the back of the cave. It seemed to the party that the remaining goblins were likely aware they were about to be attacked. 

The party split their forces with [[Evette]], [[Violet]] and [[Nura]] attacking the front of the cave while Grizzick and [[Kharmon]] held the secret exit in case the goblins attempted to escape. As the three attempted to attack the cave their plan involved [[Evette]] fake tripping as she entered. As they attacked the three met little resistance from the two goblins guarding the cave entrance. 

The goblins and their leader, expecting an attack, had planned to flee from the start. The two goblins at the entrance had merely been a delaying tactic. The human and the goblins made their way through the secret exit where they were ambushed by Grizzick and [[Kharmon]]. 

The goblins were bottlenecked for a moment but managed to push past Grizzick and [[Kharmon]]. The human used misty step to make his way outside of the cave. The rest of the party rushed through the caves to pursue the fleeing goblins but before they arrived [[Kharmon]] was already unconscious. Grizzick managed to use [[Kharmon]]'s potion to bring him back up but the goblins and the wizard could already sense the tables turning and decided to continue the fight rather than flee. 

The party managed to kill many of the remaining goblins but the wizard was able to use the spell ice knife to devastating effect, bypassing [[Evette]]'s damage resistance. Soon the fight was over, with Grizzick and [[Nura]] killed and the rest of the party unconscious as the session came to a close.
