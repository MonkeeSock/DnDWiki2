 The party reunited at their campsite back below the tree line of the Breakrock Mountains. Aramil thanked the party for rescuing him and also explained that he had been infested with orc eggs. [[Percy]] was easily able to cure the affliction and he agreed to lead the party to the shrine.

The party had a conversation with [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] about mortality who was feeling stressed and glum about coming so close to death and feeling time pass around him. Each member of the party offered their reasoning for continuing on despite the existential dread of death. He connected in particular with [[Violet]]'s perspective. 

Overnight when [[Violet]] was on watch the ranger and rogue the party had manipulated during [[Ackriel Island Session 10]]. She caught them before they could totally sneak up the party and they explained they had been hired to chase down [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]]. Some members of the party woke to the conversation and they convinced the other party to continue negotiations in the morning. [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] had stolen a magical necklace from their client, which he had used to produce the fireball in [[Ackriel Island Session 14]]. This was the last bead on the necklace and therefore he could not return it. The rival party's paladin insisted he face trial for his thievery. [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] agreed to return in a year and a day to face trial and [[Percy]] agreed to help retrieve him if he did not show up. 

Aramil led the party to the shrine where he and Wren departed. The party protected [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] from a horde of orcs and cultists who tried to disrupt his ritual to restore his divinity. In the end the ritual was successful and [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] summoned a murder of crows and used his own blade to help defeat the remaining horde. He gave the party one of his feather and a silver bell they could use three times to summon him into combat before returning to the [[Feywild]].
