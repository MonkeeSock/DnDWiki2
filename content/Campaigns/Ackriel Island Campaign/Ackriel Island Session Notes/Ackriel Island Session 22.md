Olivia instructed the party how to find the scroll where she had buried it outside of the town limits. As the party was approaching where it was hidden the party realized they were being followed. They rounded on the person following them and found it was a teenager who called himself Shadow, but was really named Tyler. 

Shadow explained he had been hired by [[Alorra]] to follow the party and confirm they had recovered the scroll. He was then supposed to return to her to help her set up an ambush for the party. The party paid Shadow to return to [[Alorra]] and tell her they had taken a different route back.

After recovering the scroll the party made their way back to [[Gödun]]. Along he road they saw a body crumpled on the ground. It was Shadow with [[Alorra]] standing above him. She explained that Shadow was a bad liar and she and her men had beaten him but not killed him. [[Alorra]] was willing to make a deal with the party if they destroyed the scroll. Under the agreement made [[Alorra]] was prevented from interfering with the party for four months, after which she could not attack the party unless in self defense and not override their free will. 

After they made a deal Allora explained that she had a job for the party. She believed a demon had been summoned in the city and wanted the party to locate it. If they needed help defeating it she would assist but would pay the party more if they took care of the issue themselves. She had her own business to attend to in town. 

After [[Alorra]] left the party helped Shadow to his feet and learned more about his background. They gave him some money and letter of introduction for [[Baron Movitch]] and sent him to [[Nisk]] where he could find some gainful employment. 

The party went to track the demon [[Alorra]] had hired them to find. As they searched for leads they heard rumors of a person who had gone missing on Dubrech Island and a griffon which had been spotted in the mountains outside of town. 

They investigated the missing person who was a local bake. His employee, Eamond, was the last person who saw him. They spoke to Eamond and learned he had accidently released a powerful demon he found trapped in a locket among his dead grandfather's things. To appease the demon he had led his boss to it as a sacrifice.

The party left Eamond and decided they needed help, but wanted help from someone other than [[Alorra]].
