The party returned to Toril's Hall and [[Knura]] used water breathing to enable the party to explore the submerged fortress. [[Violet]] turned her familiar into an angler fish to enable [[Percy]] to see in the dark water. 

On the first floor they found a tunnel leading through the collapsed sink hole of the throne room and the exterior gate of the fortress. They could see movement in the arrow slits along the wall of the entrance. The party chose to pass through the gate over the tunnel where they were attacked be Sahugain. The Sahugain used the arrow slits to their advantage but the party was still able to defeat most of them so the survivors fled to the next floor, before the party could pursue them.

As they entered the fortress proper the first room they entered was an underground courtyard behind the gate. The courtyard was crammed the bodies of dwarves who tried to escape the fortress but had been trapped inside by the gate and drowned. In the cold, salty water their bodies were pale, wrinkled and well preserved. The party pushed their way through the floating corpses and into the next floor. As the DM writes this he is realizing there was a missed opportunity to have these dwarves spring back as undead, how creepy would that have been? Next time.

On the second floor the party found some of the ancient living quarters with an old couple in one who had refused to flee, hands still locked together as they had been when they drowned there centuries ago. They found and killed the remaining Sahugain in a guard tower and continued to the third floor. 

On the third floor they found where the throne room had collapsed to and a tunnel leading to the final chamber. This camber was a dome held up by Toril himself who used the last of his magic centuries ago to enlarge himself and turn himself to stone to keep the hall from sinking completely. In the middle of the dome was his crown, guarded by a water elemental waiting for someone to attempt to take the crown. The party positioned themselves around the elemental and attacked swiftly defeating it, taking the crown and returning to the surface.
