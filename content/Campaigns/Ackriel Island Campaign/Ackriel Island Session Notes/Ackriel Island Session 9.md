As the dust settled and the party recovered their wits after the explosion [[Maldar]] stood to his full height after days of being chained to a wall. In a last ditch effort he told the party to walk away, but they refused. [[Maldar]] moved first, casting Hunger of Hadar in the hall just outside of the cell, blinding and restricting he movement of the party in an already tight corridor. Next, he summoned his pact weapon to his hand before beginning to climb over the rubble and out of his cell. 

As [[Maldar]] began to climb out of the dungeon a few imps disguised as rats moved to attack the captain of the guard, Fineshit, who had unlocked the cell door.  In the cell across from [[Maldar]] there was another priosner, [[Shrimp]], who the captain offered freedom to if they helped take down [[Maldar]]. [[Shrimp]] darted into [[Maldar]]'s cell and began attacking the imps. 

[[Violet]] followed [[Shrimp]] into the cell but this was all she was able to do, using all of her resources to push through the spell and crowded hallway. [[Kharmon]] attempted to make his own way into the cell but found it too corwded as he stumbled through cold darkness of Hunger of Hadar and instead began to make his way out through the prison's usual entrance. 

The guards attempted to push into the cell but were unable to. Captain Fineshit managed to stay standing but the two guards with her fell to [[Maldar]]'s spell. She also began to cut at the imps in the cell. Cultists began pouring into the cell from the hole [[Maldar]] was crawling through. 

[[Knura]] began her turn by turning into a giant spider and entering the cell on the ceiling. Most critically, she was able to en-web [[Maldar]], restraining him and keeping him from moving until he escaped the web. Finally, [[Evette]] went and pushed her way into the cell. With the extra reach on her halberd she was able to target [[Maldar]], the cultists and the imps. 

As combat continued the imps targeted the captain and the rest of the party, forcing [[Knura]] out of her spider form with their poison stingers. [[Maldar]] was unable to escape the web and had his concentration broken on the Hunger of Hadar by [[Violet]].
