The ship arrived in Starkovis and the party followed the captain into the city. They tracked him to a warehouse in the city where the gunpowder was being sifted and given to [[Alorra]]. The party found a few local paladins of the city to help stop the plot.

They entered the warehouse and confronted Alorra and her her thugs. The captain sided with the party and his crew fled. They used their decanter of endless water to ruin the gunpowder before surrounding her and nearly killing her. Allora barely managed to escape by using dimension door to teleport away. 

They learned she was trying to casue an explosion on the city to be blamed on [[Gödun]] rebels and escalate the conflict.
