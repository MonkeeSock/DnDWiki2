After fleeing from Alarra the party went to [[Baron Movitch]] to inform him of [[Lord Ivor]]'s death. The baron assembled some guards to secure the manor with the party. Alarra left a note, taunting [[Evette]].

They searched the rest of the manor and found a forged gold coin in the cracks on the floor of Olivia's bedroom. [[Lady Dalianna]] met with them and gave them a letter she had received which had been sent to [[Maldar]] but long delayed in its delivery. The letter was sealed the crest of a noble family of [[Ackriel Island]] but [[Violet]] was unable to recall exactly which family. The party also learned a little more about Olivia's background. 

The next day the party attended the celebrations for [[Tsarn]]'s Day, on the summer solcitce. After attending the opening ceremony they watched the parade. Many noble families participated in the parade, including one with a crest matching the seal on Olivia's letter. When of his horses broke lose and [[Knura]] was able to calm the horse and join him during the parade with Violet. They leared he was Lord Stulton from [[Gödun]]. He had been kicked out of town during the revolt. 

Next they went to the market to investigate the forged coin. While in the market they met a vendor selling "genuinely" blessed sun tea. The party saw the acolyte blessing the sun tea and were able to quickly determine the whole thing was a scam. They met [[Shrimp]]'s contact who explained the coin likely came from [[Gödun]].

Last, the party went to the harbor to watch and then participate in the boat race there. They met Olivia's brother there who was reluctant to talk about Olivia largely because another women. [[Alorra]], had already been asking about her. Once the party convinced him they did not have bad intentions he explained a little bit about where Olivia might have gone indicating she might have gone to [[Gödun]]. 

The party participated in the boat race and were pulling into first just behind Olivia's brother. As they passed he capsized, the party stopped to rescue him and still won the race. He gave them a a necklace to show Olivia once they found her. 

The party traveled to [[Gödun]] and found Olivia in a tavern. [[Percy]] and [[Violet]] approached first but a drunk women bumped into them and began to point out there noble origins. The crowd began to get riled up but they noticed this was likely [[Alorra]] disguised with an illusion. They were able to diffuse the situation and the party and [[Alorra]] spoke to Olivia together. [[Alorra]] offered her a huge sum of money. The party used the necklace her brother had given them to convince her to give the scroll to them. Once [[Alorra]] left she explained she had hidden the scroll somewhere and tell the party where it was.
