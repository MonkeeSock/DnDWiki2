The party decided to track down the rumors of the griffon they had heard to either tame it for [[Percy]] or enlist the help of the Griffon Knight who owned it. The party tracked the rumors to a village outside of town called Halmutz and hired a carriage to bring them out there. 

They entered the mountains but were unable to track the griffon until [[Knura]] used the locate beasts spell. As they approached the griffon they saw a person had set up camp near it. [[Violet]] sent her familiar to get more details. She saw a young, handsome and refined man with dirty blonde hair. [[Percy]] recognized his description as [[Edward Darcy]].

Initially [[Percy]] approached Darcy by himself. Darcy taunted [[Percy]], in particular making fun of him for being an orphan and made a strange pass at [[Valeria Modell]]. The rest of the party emerged to back up [[Percy]]. Darcy gave the party little useful information and continued to taunt [[Percy]], othering him the entire conversation. They did learn he had come with [[Eliza Fairsail]] who had gone into [[Gödun]]. She had left Darcy here to watch the Griffon.

Fed up, [[Percy]] challenged Darcy to a  fight. They had nearly come to blows many times in the past but others had been around to break things up. Originally, it was going to be a duel with swords but instead they had a fist fight. The fight was fairly even but tipping [[Percy]]'s favor when Darcy tried to push him in the camp fire twice. [[Percy]] managed to resist being thrown thanks to his party cheering him on. 

Finally, [[Percy]] knocked out Darcy. Darcy had always made him feel alone, other and small but this time Darcy was alone, beaten and small and [[Percy]] was surrounded by friends. He yelled and cried and Valeria held him. 

[[Knura]] used a spell to speak with the Griffon and learned a little more but not much. They returned to [[Gödun]] and began asking around for Lady Fairsail. They tracked her to a tavern and asked the innkeeper for information. After a bribe they learned she was staying with an elven woman who matched [[Alorra]]'s description. 

The party waited for them to return to the tavern and surprised [[Alorra]]. The two were a little frustrated to be caught together but the party explained they needed [[Alorra]]'s help with the demon. [[Alorra]] geared up in her room with Lady Fairsail. As a cockroach [[Knura]] entered the room to see if she could get any information but learned, only seeing them kiss. 

The party went to Eamond's grandfather's house and asked him to lead them to the demon. He explained the demon was in the basement and about to take another sacrifice. The party was suspicious of Eamond and made him enter the basement sandwiched between them. As they entered they did not see the demon. Percy's divine sense did not pick up the demon until he entered the room. As soon as he entered he was able to detect the invisible demon who closed the basement door, locking the party in.

The fight began and the party focused on the demon. Eamond joined in the fight, casting spells to attack [[Knura]] and [[Evette]]. [[Alorra]] was unable to do much during the fight. Eventually the party defeated the demon with [[Evette]] stirking the final blow. [[Alorra]] then incinerated Eamond.
