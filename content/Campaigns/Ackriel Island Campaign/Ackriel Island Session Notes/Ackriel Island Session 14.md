The party continued there search through the mountains for the orcish stronghold. First they found ancient dwarvish hall. It was once the palace of Toril, an ancient dwarven king. It had since been flooded and abandoned. 

The party continued trekking through the mountains until they spotted another ruin along the ridgeline. [[Violet]] sent her familiar to scout the keep and they saw it was crawling with orcs. While they had found the orc stronghold the familiar was not able to find where the prisoners were being kept. The party sent [[Knura]] in a cockroach form to scout further. She found the prisoner cells in the basement of ruined keep's central tower.

The party formed a plan where [[Evette]] and [[Percy]] would draw some orc warriors out of the fortress by attacking and then running away. [[Violet]] turned herself invisible and [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] in his crow form snuck into the tower. [[Knura]] and Wren stayed near the fort to provide backup wherever in might be needed. The original plan had been to release Aramil, casting invisibility on him and sneaking out. Upon seeing the rest of the captives, however, [[Violet]] decided it was worth trying to rescue all of them. They emerged from the cellar with the captives behind them, in the middle of the fortress. [[Violet]], [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] and Aramil were able to quietly kill two of the guards but the third was able to alert the rest with a shout. 

As [[Violet]] and the captive began to run out of the tower, through the courtyard and to the exit [[Percy]] and [[Evette]] battled the 4 orcs who had chased them from the fortress. While they were tied up in that fight [[Knura]] and Wren made it to the gate of the fortress to support [[Violet]] and [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] in their retreat. As they fled across the courtyard the other captives were all cut down by orcs who were surrounding [[Violet]], [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] and Aramil. 

As they made it to the exit the orcs continued to pursue them, [[Knura]] was able to absorb a lot of damage and hold back some of the orcs in her bear form. Aramil fled but the rest continued to fight to ensure they all escaped. Soon, [[Violet]], Wren and [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] were in trouble. [[Evette]] and [[Percy]] were able to catch up with the rest of the party. [[Knura]] was able to keep the party up with healing word but things were looking dire. 

[[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] had a final trick up his sleeve howvere and took out a necklace with a red bead on it. He plucked the bead from the necklace and tossed it towards the orcs, producing a fireball and leaving only a few orcs still standing. He then took on his crow form and flew away. The party was able to mop up the last of the orcs and flee, meeting up with Aramil and [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]].
