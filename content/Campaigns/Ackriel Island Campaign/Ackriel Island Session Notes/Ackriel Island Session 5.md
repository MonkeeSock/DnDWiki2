The session began with each member of the party being visited by the archdevil [[Lilith]] in their dreams. She asked each of them to free [[Maldar]] but the party refused so [[Lilith]] pulled them into a nightmare. 

The party "awoke" in a small, cozy cottage with a roaring fire in the hearth and a vicious storm pounding the small windows. There was a door in the back leading to the kitchen and hatches to the cellar and attic. The party quickly realized they were still in a dream. Some members of the party began to search the cottage for supplies and break down some of the furniture to fuel the fire. Meanwhile, [[Kharmon]] wiped some condensation from one of the small windows to get a better look at their surroundings. As he wiped away the fog he found an icy, blue humanoid face looking back in at him with a hand and long fingernail scratching at the glass, asking to be let in. 

[[Kharmon]] asked the blue faced man who he was. He said his name was Krampus. The rest of the party immediately began to search for a way to secure and barricade the entrances while [[Kharmon]] continued to speak to Krampus. After being taunted by [[Kharmon]] Krampus became frustrated and began to turn into his ice devil form from his humanoid one. He scurried to the cottage roof and began to crawl down the chimney, but the fire kept him from entering the cottage. 

As the party searched for a way to escape a door appeared with three locks on it which led from the nightmare cottage to summer fields of true dreams. Now all they had to do was find the three keys.

[[Evette]] stayed in the living room to tend the fire and delay Krampus should he manage to enter. [[Kharmon]] went to the cellar, [[Violet]] to the attic and [[Knura]] to the kitchen. 

In the cellar [[Kharmon]] found it stocked with jars, provisions barrels and other such sundry. Floating in the middle of the room [[Kharmon]] saw a brass key. Taking a moment to look more closely he was able to make out the edges of a gelatinous cube which the key was suspended in. Kharmon prepared to hit the cube with a firebolt but as he did it sprang into action along with three gray oozes he had not noticed before. Kharmon used a twinned scorching ray on two of the gray oozes and soon realized they were stronger than he had originally assumed and wisely fled back upstairs. 

Meanwhile, [[Knura]] entered the kitchen and was confronted with a manifestation of [[Maldar]]. [[Maldar]] had a key on his belt and seemed unhappy to see [[Knura]]. When she explained she needed the key [[Maldar]] was reluctant to give it to her. She promised to release him from his imprisonment when she woke up but [[Maldar]] did not believe her. Unable to convince him she tried to give him a final hug or kiss as a distraction while she swiped the key. [[Maldar]] still turned her down and, like [[Kharmon]], [[Knura]] retreated from the kitchen.

Up in the attic [[Violet]] found various items in storage, most notably a piano with the phrase "thE key is hiDinG in thE AttiC". Entirely on her own, and certainly with no help from the party members who were not in the attic [[Violet]] played the E,D,G,E,A,C keys on the piano. As she hit the last key she found it was lose and made no sound. She was able to remove the piano key securing the first key the party needed to escape. 

The party regrouped in the cottage living room and agreed to deal with he cellar first. [[Kharmon]] moved into the corner of the cellar and unleashed some devastating scorching rays. [[Knura]] used moonbeam to deal some damage and [[Violet]] used a chromatic orb on the gelatinous cube. On its turn, the cube engulfed both [[Violet]] and [[Knura]] doing a large amount of damage to both. [[Kharmon]] had a vision of the gray oozes surrounding and killing him, but fortunately they're movement speed was slower than thought and they weren't able to move and hit him in one round. 

[[Evette]] had originally stayed in the living room but got involved after hearing the screams of her companions. She opened the cellar hatch and reached into the cube to pull [[Violet]] out. On [[Violet]]'s turn she pulled [[Knura]] out of the cube. [[Kharmon]] killed the rest of the gray oozes with scorching ray. He was able to regenerate some spell slots by pushing the boundaries of the dream and contesting Krampus's will. The whole party entered the basement to deal with the cube. [[Violet]] was engulfed again but they were quickly able to dispatch the monster. 

The party retrieved the second key and returned to living room. [[Knura]] was unable to face the kitchen again so she stayed to tend the fire while the rest of the party went into the back of the cottage. This time they were confronted with a manifestation of [[Violet]]'s father. He was cold, dismissive and disappointed by default. He was disapproving of [[Violet]]'s companions and she was unable to convince him to give her the key. [[Kharmon]], however, was able to turn things around promising that [[Violet]] was on a heroic quest which would bring honor to her family's name. He handed the key over after sternly reminding [[Violet]] not to embarrass her family.

With all three keys secured the party fled into their normal, sweet dreams as the fire died behind them.
