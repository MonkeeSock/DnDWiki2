The party was invited to a party in the tower overlooking the city of Starkovis. They met a variety of characters there and heard rumors of the tower being haunted.

[[Eliza Fairsail]] was present with [[Edward Darcy]]. She ordered [[Percy]] to keep silent about her relationship to [[Alorra]]. The party managed to extract a later favor from her in exchange.  

The party was honored by the Baron Imhoff after which they saw Darcy sneaking into the upper levels of the tower. [[Shrimp]] followed him and saw he was trying to steal official seals and documents. She had to sneakily help him so he could succeed and incriminate himself. 

After securing some letters and a seal of the baron's he went to the upper haunted levels of the tower and began to search there. [[Percy]] imitated a ghost in the tower to terrify Darcy, get him to drop the things he had stolen and reveal what he was there for. He was sent by Lady Fairsail to acquire the documents and seal and went to the upper levels to find a cool sword for himself, like Percy's. 

As the party went to retrieve the items they were attacked by a real spirit in the tower.
