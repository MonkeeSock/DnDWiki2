The party met the owner of a shipping company who suspected one of his captains was smuggling some sort of good and hired the party to investigate. They came on board as "passengers" on their way to Starkovis.

The ship set sail and the captain kept the party in the galley, rowing. To get out of this they bragged about finding the sword of Toril. Which impressed the captain but he only made a deal with the party once they agreed to supply fresh water with their decanter of endless water. 

The ship arrived on Tharosis Island. The captain went to pick up the cargo and [[Violet]] followed them disguised. She witnessed the captain dealing with a hobgoblin who [[Evette]] recognized as the dealer who supplied [[Maldar]] with his gunpowder.

After the cargo was loaded the party investigated and found they were mixing gunpowder in with spice to sift out later and smuggle into the city. [[Violet]] helped repair the relationship between the captain and his adoptive son more to distract them but did provide genuine help.
