Valeria is a member of the [[Order of the Griffon]] and the adopted daughter of Casimir Modell. She was sent by the order to serve as [[Percy]]'s retainer and witness as he seeks eligibility for higher rank in the order.

Valeria herself does not seek a higher position in the order. She joined [[Percy]] on her journey to explore the world and hopefully find her own goals. She is a talented swordswoman and loyal to her friends and family.
