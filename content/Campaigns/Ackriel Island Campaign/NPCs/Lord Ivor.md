Lord Ivor is the Lord of [[Muntz]]. Formarlly, he employed the wizard named [[Alkar]]. [[Alkar]] created an amulet of charm person and found a scroll of summon archdevil for Lord Ivor. At this time Ivor was a knight of lower status and he used the amulet to marry above his station, becoming a proper lord. He married Lady Dalianna, only daughter of the powerful [[Baron Movitch]]. 

Ivor also used the amulet to have an affair with [[Alkar]]'s wife, [[Olivia]]. This caused a falling out between the two and eventually led to [[Alkar]]'s death.
