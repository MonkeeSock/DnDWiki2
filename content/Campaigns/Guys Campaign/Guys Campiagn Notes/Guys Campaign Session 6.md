1/26/25
26th of New Rains


The party spent the night in [[Almarra Forest]] just outside of the cult's lair. The party each took turns at watch with [[Amaryllis]] who could not sleep that night. During [[Lucien]]'s watch a toad entered the light of the party's campfire. As [[Amaryllis]] and [[Lucien]] stared at the toad, Lucien determined it was magical just before it spoke. The toad asked Lucien if he would be visiting "mother dearest". Lucien said he would visit the next day. The rest of the night went by uneventfully. 

As the party woke in the morning they prepared to leave the forest. As they left they were followed by dozens of toads which followed the party to the very edge of the forest. Just as the party approached [[Minarra]] they were stopped by [[Reylar]] and [[Atreos]], a tiefling mercenary. [[Reylar]] explained that [[Lord Phelton]] had taken [[Smendry]] and a handful of other villagers hostage after [[Kal Rincar]] had told him about the fight in the cider mill. The lord and his men were held up in the manor on the hill.  

The party agreed to rescue [[Smendry]] and the others. They gathered intel from the villagers and learned [[Lord Phelton]] had six men with him and the general layout of the manor. The manor had three main entrances, the front door and two back doors in the garden as well as windows along the side. The party broke into three groups. [[Gris]] and [[Lucien]] approached the front door, primarily to create a distraction. [[Vammeagar]] and [[Lykopsaro]] approached one of the rear doors and [[Ronnie]], [[Sinwar Nakla Conghaile Hassan]] and [[Atreos]] approached the other backdoor. 

[[Gris]] used his noble upbringing to attempt and leverage an audience with [[Lord Phelton]] but the guards were reluctant. [[Lucien]] pressed the matter, playing up their indignation. Eventually one of the two guards at the front door went to speak with [[Lord Phelton]] about the situation.

[[Lykopsaro]] and [[Vammeagar]] were spotted during their approach and forced to halt by the guards. [[Lykopsaro]] claimed to be an envoy of a foreign land while [[Vammeagar]] claimed to be a travelling merchant. The guards by this particular door did not believe their story and ordered them to remain outside. 

[[Ronnie]] turned invisible and was able to easily sneak up to the other door with [[Sinwar Nakla Conghaile Hassan]] and [[Atreos]]. [[Ronnie]] was able to unlock the door but there was another guard standing behind it, so he and the others entered through a window instead. As they entered they saw the guard who had left to speak with [[Lord Phelton]] heading towards the basement. [[Atreos]] disguised himself as the guard and returned to the one guard remaining at the front door. He claimed that [[Lord Phelton]] had requsted [[Gris]] and [[Lucien]] were let in and once they were in she convinced the other guard to let [[Lykopsaro]] and [[Vammeagar]] in. 

With the party all inside the manor they waited for the guard to return from the cellar. [[Atreos]] stood at the top of the stairs still disguised as him as he came around the corner. Blood was smeared on his panicked face and his helmet was askew but even as he rushed up the stairs he paused upon seeing himself looking back down at him. [[Atreos]] killed him with two crossbow shots before he could say a word or shout. The party headed down the stairs to confront [[Lord Phelton]]. 

As they entered the cellar Lord Phelton shouted at them for ruining his plans and a fight began. Before Lord Phelton and his summoned iyats could act Sinwar hit the lord with a powerful shot, using the ritual circle carved into the floor to do additional damage before taking cover.

[[Gris]] and [[Lucien]] followed engaging the iyats. They were immediately followed by [[Lord Phelton]] who began slinging spells. [[Lykopsaro]] entered the fray using his channel divinity to deal some massive area damage and immediately destroy some of the weaker iyats. 

The iyats moved next, rushing forward and jamming up the entrance. The remaining iyats were each larger, tougher creatures. They started with some basic attacks against the members of the party that were still within reach. 

[[Atreos]] and [[Vammeagar]] entered the fight. [[Atreos]] did heavy damage against the iyats by dual wielding their swords and using the ritual blood drops to deal extra damage. [[Vammeagar]] used misty step to move past the iyats and engage [[Lord Phelton]] in close combat. 

The rest of the fight continued with the iyats pushing [[Lykopsaro]] into the ritual circle in order to deal additional damage to him. While [[Lykopsaro]] was soon heavily damaged it wasn't long before the party defeated the iyats and killed [[Lord Phelton]].

The party freed [[Smendry]] who had endured the worst of the torture and the rest the captured villagers. They tried to establish [[Smendry]] as the lord of the village but he was reluctant. He did agree to lead the village until a new lord was appointed by [[Count Dravish]]. The people of [[Minarra]] threw a feast for the party that night before they left the next day. As payment [[Smendry]] invited the party to loot through his manor. They found some money and a handful of magic items.
