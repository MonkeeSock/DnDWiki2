11/30/24
24th - 25th of New Rains

The party met back up with [[Lykopsaro]] and regrouped before making their way into [[Almarra Forest]] to exterminate the cult. They confirmed through [[Lykopsaro]]'s research that [[Azorack]] was an aspect of the [[Yugoloth]]. 

Guided by [[Lucien]] and [[Ronnie]] the party made their way through the forest and to the cult's hideout. As they arrived they met [[Vammeagar]], a dwarven wizard also seeking the cult's lair. Vanagar had already destroyed two iyats guarding he door. 

[[Lucien]] opened the door and [[Ronnie]] scouted the lair ahead. He found a hallway which sloped deeper into the ground, which led to a large candle lit chamber where chanting sounds echoed from. Partway down the hall there was a door on either side. [[Ronnie]] returned and informed the party of what he had seen. The party all entered the lair, quite loudly, and the cultists further inside heard the party stumbling in the dark chamber. 

After a failed attempt to pass as the captured sacrifices the party entered the main chamber. They found a circle of blood in the center of the chamber, surrounded by empty drops of blood carved into the floor. A number of cultists stood at the edges of the chamber and two of the cults leaders in the center. In the middle of the ritual was [[Amaryllis]]'s father, Cormac. Cormac had been evidently brutally tortured and a brass plate covered part of skull where he had been lobotomized. 

[[Gris]] attempted to hold [[Amaryllis]] back but failed as she rushed towards her father. There was not much more preamble, enslaved or not the cult needed sacrifices and the party would serve just as well. The battle began.

The leader of the cult, Karzick, began the fight with a lightning bolt which did serious damage to half of the party. Gris went next, entering the chamber and striking one of the cultists around its edge. Karzick then used his first villain action to summon a variety of iyats in the chamber which struck out at [[Amaryllis]] and Gris primarily. 

[[Ronnie]], [[Lucien]] and [[Vammeagar]] traded blows and spells with the cultists but as the first round drew to close the party was on the backfoot. The cultists had succeeded in resisting the parties spells and had managed to dish out some serious damage. 

Things suddenly turned around on [[Lykopsaro]]'s turn as he entered the chamber and channeled the holy power of [[Glamthring]], unleashing a devastating blast of holy fire and light. The weaker cultists and several of the iyats were immediately immolated. This cleared all of the weakest enemies from the battle immediately, allowing the party to focus on Karzick and his toughest minions. 

As Karzick's turn came around again he unleashed another lighting bolt on several of the party members, forcing them to reduce the damage and fill some of the empty blooddrops on the floor. As the fight continued Karzick and his allies managed to fill many of the blood drops themselves, with Karzick in particular moving around the map to keep near empty blood drops. 

During the fight [[Lucien]] made use of command to force Karzick to drop his wand which Gris retrieved. This also cost Karzick an entire turn, preventing him from doing some serious damage to the party. [[Vammeagar]] ended up going toe-to-toe with Karzick's most powerful remaining ally, avoiding a potentially devastating inflict wounds. 

[[Gris]], [[Ronnie]] and [[Amaryllis]] worked on clearing the remaining iyats while [[Lykopsaro]] provided much needed healing. Eventually Karzick forced [[Gris]] to drop the wand as well but before he could retrieved it [[Vammeagar]] broke away from the cultist and used mage hand to retrieve the wand herself. As Karzick became surrounded and continued to fail saving throws due to [[Lucien]]'s hex he was forced to use his second villain action to reposition and break the concentration on the spell. 

The remaining iyats were destroyed, but at this point the party was badly injured and [[Gris]] was dropped by the remaining cult fanatic. [[Lykopsaro]] healed Griss but immediately after Karzick used his final villainous action to unleash a devestating psychic attack on the party, forcing them to relive all of their most painful and traumatic memories in an instant. Several of the party members were dropped, and [[Vammeagar]] would have been outright killed had she not filled a blood drop to lessen the damage. 

The party members who were still standing, [[Amaryllis]] and [[Ronnie]] used potions to bring up the unconscious party members who brought still more back into fighting shape. The party was on their last legs, but so were the cultists and Karzick had no more such tricks up his sleeves. The cult fanatic was dispatched and Karzick used Arms of Hadar to move around the battlefield and fill the ritual's blood drops. In the last round Gris hit Karzick hard with a melee attack before [[Lykopsaro]] finished him with his fire breath. 

The cult was defeated and only three of the twenty-five drops required for the ritual remained unfilled. Though a close margin, this was enough to prevent [[Azorack]] from being summoned. [[Amaryllis]] ran to her father's side, who had been killed as a result of Karzick's second villain action, and clung to his body while she wept. 

The party gave her space to grieve and examined the lair. They determined that this ritual was likely to be the first half of the summoning and that by preventing this half from being completed they had effectively blocked the second half. They explored the rest of the lair finding the cult's barracks and Karzick's office. They also found a torture chamber attached to the office, with other deceased victims still inside. The party searched the office leaning that in order to completely prevent the ritual they would have to find the second half and drain it, similar to how this one had been filled. [[Vammeagar]] found a package with arcane seals on it which she pocketed for herself. 

After lotting the valuables the furniture and bodies were piled in the chamber and set ablaze by [[Vammeagar]]. The party sealed the door and made camp in [[Almarra Forest]] before seeking the other half of the ritual.
