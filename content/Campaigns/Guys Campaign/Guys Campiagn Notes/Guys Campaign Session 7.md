The party returned from [[Minarra]] to [[Iartomita]]. As they disembarked from [[The Maiden]] they were greeted by [[Captain Tallanvor]] who explained the party was being investigated and needed to stay in the city for the time being. He also wanted the party to meet him at a place called Merwin's Fine Inks and Pens. He gave them a small pin of a white rose.

The party arrived and explained they were friends of [[Captain Tallanvor]]. Upon showing Merwin the pin they were led into the back where they met Noodle, Grog, Cadaric and Anders. After making heir introductions Anders explained he was reforming the Order of the White Rose. The Order had been nearly wiped out years ago and much of its records and books taken by cultists. Anders suspected some may now be stored in the Temple of [[Lifttan]].

The party left with their new members and went to drink at Judd's. [[Lucien]], [[Lykopsaro]] and [[Gris]] got wasted. They began to make their way home and got lost. [[Atreos]] had to lead them back home. As they made their way home she saw a single eye watching them from the rooftop. 

The next morning [[Atreos]] left early to visit the temple and research the eye. The rest of the party had heard that [[Pevarra]]'s shop had been robbed. Speaking with her they learned someone had broken, cracked her safe and stolen spell scrolls. The scrolls that were stolen were ideal for infiltrating a secure place. As they investigated the outside of the building they saw claw marks leading to the roof. They made their way up and Grog, [[Gris]] and Noodle followed the tracks along the rooves. The rest of the party followed along the ground. 

Meanwhile, as [[Atreos]] made their way to the temple. Before they did they spoke to Anders about the eye they saw, learning it may be a nothic. On the way to the temple they caught a pickpocket trying to lift some coins from them. He caught them and recovered the coins. He then hired the thief to come with him to the temple in case he needed any backup. Arriving at the temple he spoke to the dwarven woman, Ingra Silverheart, at the front desk about access to the restricted and forbidden parts of the library. 

Realizing he did not have access [[Atreos]] had the thief cause a distraction who began to tear up some books. The guard at the door came to stop him and [[Atreos]] slipped into the restricted section. He was unable to find any information about the nothic in this section and unable to access the forbidden section. [[Atreos]] left and met up with the rest of the party who had tracked the thief at [[Pevarra]]'s in that direction. 

[[Lucien]] was able to get the party into the restricted section and [[Atreos]] disguised himself as Ingra to get into the forbidden section. He learned he still needed a pass to enter but managed to bluff past the guard to get in this time. He found a book wit hsome information on Nothics but not everything they needed. The book they found did reference other books that might be enough. 

To find more information they went to [[Ithamon Tower]]. They were granted access by using a letter of itnorduction from [[Vammeagar]]. As the party entered the tower they saw [[Calum Quaide Kincaid]] watching a variety of scenes in a series of crystal balls, including a scene of a man and woman lying in bed together. Calum provided the party with the book they were looking for, learning enough about Nothics.

The party deduced the nothics lair might be in the city's clock tower, built by [[Hen Ymorda]]. The tower had no entry at its base so Grog tried to climb up the tower. Grog nearly fell but managed to catch himself. He found a secret door built into the towers wall and punched through it to climb in. He then lowered a rope to get the rest of the party in

Cadaric snuck to the top of the tower and found the nothic there. They spoke to it and convinced it to take a piece of [[Lucien]]'s hair so they could spy on it later. They made a dealt to meet with it later and exchange information. The party left and [[Lucien]] treid to spy om the nothic, unfortunately gaining no information. 

The party went to meet the nothic and Grog was sent to investigate its lair while it was away at the meeting. In the alley they were supposed to meet it they were instead met by temple authorities and the archpriest searching for Noodle. The nothic had stolen a secret from Noodle and exposed it to temple authorities. The party managed to force them to retreat and interrogated one of the acolytes. 

Meanwhile Grog entered the tower and found the nothic there. Realizing they had been betrayed he attacked it. During the fight the nothic tried to use telekinesis to throw Grog out the window but failed and was forced to use dimension door to escape. 

The next day the party again tried to track it, finding an abandoned apartment where they had seen the scene of the man and woman sleeping together. They then managed to track it through the sewers to the temple. 

The party stole a pass from Ingra and attempted to forge a copy of the pass then returned it. [[Lucien]], and [[Atreos]] used illusory passes with Cadaric's forged one to try and get into the forbidden section. The guard saw through the passes and ordered the party to wait there while other temple authorities arrived. Pretending to need to smoke by the window they each slipped out the window and escaped. 

They returned to the temple undisguised and told Ingra someone was trying to break in. she believed they had already caught the intruders since the party had just fled. [[Lucien]] was able to convince Ingra to let them into the forbidden section to check anyways. 

Ingra told the party do as she said as they made their way through. They found the nothic inside and a fight started. When the nothic used the spell lightening bolt it summoned an air elemental. The nothic was killed but the elemental killed Ingra. [[Gris]] and fled to the with her body in the hopes of being able to revive her. The rest of the party defeated the elemental and when they exited the forbidden section they found the temple's archpriest had revived Ingra. She helped smooth the incident over.
