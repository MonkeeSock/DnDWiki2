Domains: Trickery, Nature

Symbol: Raven with the sun in its mouth

Bio: A True Elf (see [[True Elves]]) servant of [[Val]], Raven Who Steals the Sun is known for having the ability to take the form of a raven and is known to be in this form more than her elven form. She is best known for stealing shiny things, including once stealing the sun from [[Tsarn]]. She and [[Crow Whose Every Feather is a Thousand Stories]] are sometimes lovers and sometimes rivals. 

Worship: Temples dedicated to Val are often dedicated to a True Elf as well. One of the ways to honor Raven Who Steals the Sun is to gather shiny things and small trinkets that bring you joy.
