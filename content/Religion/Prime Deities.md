There are eight Prime Deities worshipped on [[The Material Plane]]. These are the survivors of [[The War of the Gods|/Calamities/The War of the Gods]], a series of calamitous battles that took place across the Material Plane. Many other Gods were killed (see [[Dead Gods|/DeadGods/Dead Gods]]), exiled and banished during this period. Some became leaders of [[Demon Lords]]
and others [[content/Religion/Archdevils/Archdevils|Archdevils]]. In general, the Prime Deities remain uninvolved in the affairs of the Material Plane preferring to enact change via indirect means. This is due to the fact that they are neither omnipotent nor omniscient in addition certain bindings that were placed on them after the War of the Gods. 

Worship of the Prime Deities is as varied as it is widespread. There is no codified form of worship. Different cultures across [[The Material Plane]] worship them in many different ways and sometimes ascribe different meanings, roles and attributes to the various deities. There are certain motifs that hold true across all cultures while other aspects may be more or less emphasized or even entirely absent. For example, [[Elves]] may emphasize [[Teitheo]]'s relationship to music while [[Dwarves]] worship his role in creation as a whole including sculpture and in some cases even birth under his domain. Some culture's may draw on [[Weitheo]]'s power for fortune telling while others may call upon [[Elslyn]].
