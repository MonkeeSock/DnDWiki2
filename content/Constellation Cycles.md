The constellations on [[The Material Plane]] change from year to year through four distinct cycles. 

The first cycle is known as *The Divine Cycle* and features constellations of holy figures such as the [[Prime Deities]], the metallic [[Dragons]], significant heroes etc. It is generally believed good luck and fortune are more common during this cycle as well as better harvests.

The second cycle is *The Material Cycle*. It contains constellation of various things common to [[The Material Plane]], like plants, animals and household objects. This cycle is the one least associated with superstitions and is generally considered to be a neutral cycle. 

The third cycle is *The Ruin Cycle* and contains constellations featuring [[Devils]], [[Demons]] and other fiends. Renowned villains and vicious monsters also frequently feature in this cycle. This cycle is associated with bad harvests, ill luck and general fear and destruction. 

The fourth and final cycle is *The Chaos Cycle* which contains a random assortment of constellations from the other three cycles. In theory, it is impossible to predict what constellations will appear during a chaos cycle. In some years this cycle predominately includes constellations from one cycle and in others the mixture is more equivalent. It is possible for completely new and unique constellations to appear during this cycle. Sometimes these new constellations become a permanent fixture in the other cycles when they are relevant. This cycle is the one associated with the most superstition and the interpretations vary widely from year to year.
